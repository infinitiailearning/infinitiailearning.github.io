Course

Applied Data Science for Industry: From Fundamentals to Deep Learning

This course provides a thorough exploration of fundamental concepts and their practical use cases in industries, with each topic being accompanied by detailed hands-on implementation. The course will be facilitated by a team of industry and academic experts and researchers, who will also be available to address any additional queries or concerns. By enrolling in this course, you will be equipped with the skills and knowledge necessary to apply for a variety of data-focused roles, including Data Scientist, Data Engineer, Data Analyst, and ML Engineer, across various industries.


Who is this course for

Beginners

If you are a beginner in data science and want to learn the foundation by working on hands-on projects.

Career Switch

If you work in industry and want to switch your career to data science. Start your journey today with us.

Academic Projects

If you want to use data science algorithms in your academic/research or personal projects.

Simply Curious

If you are not from the computer science or related discipline but are curious about the scope and application.


What you'll get out of this course

  • Video + Live Lectures + Doubt Sessions
    We offer a dynamic learning experience through our online live classes, accompanied by the convenience of video lecture recordings. This allows you the flexibility to learn and review the material at your preferred pace. Moreover, we prioritize your understanding by hosting interactive doubt-clearing sessions, ensuring a strong grasp of the fundamental concepts.
  • Solve real industrial problesm with Data Science
    As you progress through this course, you will attain the ability to skillfully determine the most appropriate algorithm for a given industrial problem. By course completion, you'll possess the insights needed to make informed and strategic algorithmic choices in an industrial context.
  • End to End training of an ML Model
    You will gain the capability to train a model tailored to your specific requirements and demands. Additionally, you'll acquire the proficiency to enact fundamental modifications within algorithms, empowering you with the freedom to fine-tune and adapt them as needed.
  • Understand Data Engineering
    You will not only learn the role of data engineering and data understanding in the realm of machine learning, but you will also grasp their pivotal significance. This understanding will enable you to navigate the intricate interplay between data and machine learning with a heightened level of expertise.

Course Syllabus

  • Introduction and Preliminaries
    • Defining Data Science and the Role of Data Scientists.
    • Importance of Big Data and its Applications
    • Fundamentals of SQL.
    • Mathematics for Data Science
      • Linear Algebra:Vectors and Matrices, Operations on Vectors and Matrices, and Eigenvalues, Eigenvectors, and SVD.
      • Probability and Statistics: Bayes Rule, Gaussian Distribution, Expectation, Variance, and Moments.
      • Calculus: Differential and Integral Calculus and Multivariate extensions.
  • Python Programming and PyTorch for Machine Learning
    • Python Programming Basics: Functions, Variables, and Libraries
    • Libraries for Data Science: Pandas, Numpy, and Scikit-learn
    • PyTorch Framework
      • Setting up PyTorch
      • Basic Statistical Analysis with PyTorch
      • PyTorch for Machine Learning examples
  • Gradient Descent and Stochastic Gradient Descent
    • Convex and non-convex Functions
    • Convex and Non-convex Optimization
    • Gradient Descent and Stochastic Gradient Descent
  • Machine Learning Algorithms for Industry
    • Introduction to Machine Learning
      • Loss, Cost, and Activation Functions
      • Bias vs. Variance
    • Supervised Machine Learning
      • Regression: Linear Regression, kNN regression and Kernel Regression
      • Classification: kNN Classification, Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression, SVM and Decision Trees
      • Bagging/Boosting: Bagging and Boosting, Random Forest, XG Boost
    • Unsupervised Machine Learning
      • Clustering: K-means and Hierarchical Clustering
      • Dimensionality Reduction: PCA and Non-linear Dimensionality Reduction
    • Reinforcement Learning
      • Introduction to RL
      • Dynamic Programming and Q-learning
  • Introduction to Deep Learning and Deep Neural Networks
    • Introduction to Artificial Neural Networks
      • Fully connected shallow neural networks
      • Backpropagation and demo
    • Convolutional Neural Networks
    • Recurrent Neural Networks
    • Advanced Neural Network Architectures
